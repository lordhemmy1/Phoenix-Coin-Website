Welcome to Phoenix Coin ($PHNX)

A Deflationary Cryptocurrency for a Decentralized Future

Key Features

Deflationary Mechanism

With every transaction, tokens are burned to increase scarcity and value.

Liquidity Generation

1% of every transaction is added to liquidity pools, ensuring stability and reducing slippage.

Community Governance

Token holders decide on key governance matters through a DAO structure.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 PHNX

Burn Mechanism: 2% per transaction split into 1% burn and 1% liquidity.

Max Burn Cap: 50% of total supply (500 million PHNX).
