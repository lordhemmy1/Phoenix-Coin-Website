About Phoenix Coin

Phoenix Coin (PHNX) is a deflationary cryptocurrency designed to offer long-term value through token burns, liquidity generation, and community-driven governance. Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) using the BEP-20 standard, PHNX offers a low-cost, scalable solution for decentralized finance (DeFi) and Web3 applications.

With a unique deflationary mechanism, Phoenix Coin fosters long-term holding, liquidity stability, and decentralized growth. Join the PHNX ecosystem as we rise above volatility and create lasting value in the crypto space.

Key Features

Deflationary Model

1% of every transaction is burned, reducing supply and driving value.

Liquidity Generation

1% of each transaction is added to the liquidity pool, ensuring stability.

Community Governance

Holders can propose and vote on future upgrades and partnerships.

Tokenomics

Key Details

  • Total Supply: 1 Billion PHNX
  • 1% Burn Fee: Reduces supply with each transaction.
  • 1% Liquidity Fee: Enhances trading stability.
  • Max Burn Cap: 50% of total supply.
