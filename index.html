About Phoenix Coin

Phoenix Coin (PHNX) is a deflationary cryptocurrency built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), designed to offer long-term value through token burns, liquidity generation, and community-driven governance.

Tokenomics

Total Supply

1,000,000,000 PHNX

Burn Mechanism

2% Fee per transaction: 1% burned, 1% added to liquidity.

Max Burn Cap

50% of total supply will be burned.
