About Phoenix Coin

Phoenix Coin ($PHNX) is a deflationary, community-driven cryptocurrency built for long-term growth. Inspired by the mythical phoenix, it represents resilience and sustainability in the decentralized economy.

Key Features

Deflationary Model

1% of every transaction is burned, reducing supply and driving value.

Liquidity Generation

1% of each transaction is added to the liquidity pool, ensuring stability.

Community Governance

Holders can propose and vote on future upgrades and partnerships.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1 Billion PHNX

  • 1% Burn Fee: Reduces supply with each transaction.
  • 1% Liquidity Fee: Enhances trading stability.
  • Max Burn Cap: Burn stops at 50% of total supply.

Use Cases

  • Peer-to-Peer Payments: Low fees for seamless transactions.
  • DeFi Integration: Participate in staking, yield farming, and liquidity pools.
  • dApp Ecosystem: Ideal for gaming, NFTs, and digital services.

Security & Transparency

  • Built on the secure BEP-20 standard.
  • Thoroughly audited smart contracts.
  • Liquidity locking for added trust.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Contract Deployment, Marketing, DEX Listings.
  • Phase 2: Community Governance, Staking, Partnerships.
  • Phase 3: DeFi Integration, dApp Expansion, CEX Listings.
  • Phase 4: DAO Implementation, Ecosystem Growth.
